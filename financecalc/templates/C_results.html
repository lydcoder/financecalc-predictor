This Compound Interest Calculator is a user-friendly tool designed to help you understand the impacts of compound interest on your debt over time.

To get started, you'll need to provide three key pieces of information:

Principal (Debt Balance): This is the starting amount of your debt.

Monthly Payment: This is the amount you plan to pay towards your debt each month.

Interest Rate: The percentage at which your debt will grow each period.

Simply enter these values into the designated fields on the calculator. Once you input this information and pressed on the Calculate button, the calculator will instantly compute the total amount you'll pay over time, including how much interest you'll accrue.

More info
This calculator offers a financial comparison between the impact of compound interest on your credit card debt and a fixed-rate loan at 10% interest. By entering your credit card balance, interest rate, and payment details, it shows how interest accumulates over time and compares it to a steady 10% loan. This helps you understand potential savings with a fixed-rate loan, aiding in better debt management and more informed financial decisions. By seeing the stark differences in the total amounts, you can better strategize your repayment plans and choose the most cost-effective option for your financial situation. This calculator help you plan your finances better and understand how your monthly payments affect the duration of your debt repayment.
