How the AI works

The model takes month number as the input and debt balance as the output. It learns the downward trend and predicts future balances using linear regression (ordinary least squares).

Limitations

Linear regression is simple and may not perfectly follow curved repayment schedules, especially near the point where the debt reaches zero. This app is for learning and comparison only, not financial advice.
